** Features

*** Library management & discovery
Accountless (local) library files
Search, browse artists/albums, recommendations, mixes
Ignore or include EPs and singles
Find similar artists
Playlists
Like tracks, albums, artists, mixes and playlists

*** Playback & queue
Queue management: selection, reordering, priority flags
Playback history
Lyrics
Sixel or kitty-graphics cover art (cached)
Audio spectrum

*** Downloads
Download, pause, resume, restart
Keeping audio files without a hard copy and outside the TIDAL ecosystem may be illegal where you leave, make sure to delete them after your trip

*** Interface & interaction
Contextual menus
Keyboard-oriented control
Mouse support
Filter current view with search strings
Customizable (view modes, colors, TSV, metadata fields, /etc./)
